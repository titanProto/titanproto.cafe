ari

she/they

ari is my main fursona and who i consider to be the truest reflection of myself.
i don't really need to tell y'all much about her, she's me! but i certainly can tell you a little about her creation >:3
for a little while now i knew that i wanted a deer sona... well actually i was sorta between a deer and an otter for a while, but eventually i settled on a deer as the species and yes she's just a regular deer, not a reindeer, even though she has antlers. trans-gen-deer if you will :3
she was drawn by MaxineVEVO. and honestly maxine did a phenominal job turning my half-baked ideas of what i wanted in to something that i'm so happy with now. at first i was gonna make a 'natural' coloured deer, but once maxine had made her up with natural colours, i asked if it'd be possible to see her in ourple, and i fucking loved it!
some advice for anyone who is comissioning a ref sheet (or any art) for the first time (and is having some trouble getting their ideas in to words) would be to make a jamboard, like mine here.
