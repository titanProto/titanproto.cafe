currently, i'm creating a watch of sorts. it consits of a raspberry pi zero 2 w, and an inkypi black, red, and white e-ink display. this project is currently very much in the testing phase, i intend to add a battery (including charging and usb c), physical buttons and controls of some sort, and of course a case.
one day i'd like to add much more functionality to the project, but i'm just not sure what i can realistically add with the low refresh-rate of my e-ink display. the manufacturer quotes an approx a 15 second refresh rate, but in my experience it's more like 18. the black and white seem to refresh within that 15 seconds, so i assume their testing might have been only with their black+white display.
i'm thinking the extent of this will probably just be a basic-ish watch and stuff, i plan on making a fully-fledged pipboy/cyberdeck once i get a pi 5 (at which point i'll be able to repurpose my older pi 3 (the pi 4 will still likely be in use elsewhere))
